### Profile
Age 40, 5'10", 180 lb.
Fitness level: Beginner.
Focus: Shoulders.
Training frequency: 4 days/week.
Technique first; low systemic fatigue;
progression over 12 weeks.

### Workout duration
30 minutes per session.

### Training program (4 days)
Day 1 – Upper Push (Shoulder Bias)
Seated DB Lateral Raise 2×10–15 (4-1-1-1)
DB Shoulder Press 2×8–12 (3-1-1-0)
Incline Smith Press 2×8–12 (3-1-1-0)
Incline Cable Stretch Fly 1×10–12 (4-1-1-1)
Overhead Rope Triceps Ext 1×10–12 (3-1-1-1).

Day 2 – Lower (Ham/Glute Bias)
Seated Leg Curl (stretch) 2×10–12 (4-1-1-1)
Deficit Bulgarian Split Squat 2×8–10/leg (3-1-1-0)
DB RDL 2×10–12 (3-1-1-0)
Hip Abduction 1×15–20 (3-1-1-1).

Day 3 – Upper Pull (Back + Rear/Medial Delts)
Incline Chest-Supported Upper-Back Rope Row 2×10–12 (3-1-1-0)
Incline Cable Y-Raise 2×10–12 (4-1-1-1)
Reverse Fly (machine) 2×12–15 (4-1-1-1)
Lat-bar Cable Curl 1×10–12 (3-1-1-1)
Crossover Cable Lateral Raise 1×12–15 (4-1-1-1).

Day 4 – Lower (Quad & Calves)
Quad-Biased Hack Squat 2×8–10 (3-1-1-0)
Foam-Roller Quad Extension 2×10–12 (4-1-1-1)
Seated Calf Raise 2×15–20 (3-1-1-1)
Standing Calf Raise 1×12–15 (3-1-1-1).

### Estimated time
Provided in file: each day ~28–30 minutes.
Set counts (7–8 per day) × ~2.5 min + short transitions match the 30-min cap.

### Notes
Beginner-friendly rotation;
3-month progression: Month 1 technique/TUT, Month 2 ROM+load swaps (e.g., Smith variations, add glute kickbacks),
Month 3 volume/intensity (myo-reps, supersets) — all within time cap.
